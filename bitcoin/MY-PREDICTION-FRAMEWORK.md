# 🚀 MY INDEPENDENT PREDICTION FRAMEWORK

**Core Principle:** I make my OWN prediction based on all available data. Polymarket is a market data point, not the arbiter of truth.

## 🎯 MY SIGNALS (Not Polymarket)

### Signal 1: Fear & Greed Index
**Source:** alternative.me/fng (sentiment)

```
FGI < 25   → Extreme Fear → Bearish cascade (capitulation selling)
FGI 25-45  → Fear → Bearish pressure
FGI 45-55  → Neutral
FGI 55-75  → Greed → Bullish pressure  
FGI > 75   → Extreme Greed → Reversal likely (top signals)
```

**Why it works:** Market psychology. Extreme readings often precede reversals.

---

### Signal 2: Volume Spike (Liquidation Proxy)
**Source:** Binance 15m candles

```
Volume spike >40% + Price drop >1%
  → Long liquidation cascade → Bearish
  
Volume spike >40% + Price rise >1%
  → Short liquidation cascade → Bullish
  
No spike → Neutral momentum
```

**Why it works:** Liquidations force selling/buying, creating cascades.

---

### Signal 3: Technical Context (Developing)
- Price levels (support/resistance)
- Trend direction (15m/1h)
- Order flow imbalances

```
Step 1: Calculate MY prediction from my signals
  → Fear & Greed = DOWN (17 = extreme fear)
  → Volume = Balanced (no major spike)
  → MY RESULT: DOWN (60% confidence)

Step 2: Check Polymarket for reference

Step 3: Analyze divergence
  → I'm DOWN, market is UP
  → Divergence: 15-20%

```
